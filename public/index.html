About Us Our Services

Door to Door International Logistics Management,
with a Hands on Approach

Greystones Maritime International (GMI) is a logistics management company headquartered in Dulles, Virginia, adjacent to Dulles International Airport in Washington, DC.

Whether it be by barge, road, or rail, we add value to your logistics operation by:

  • Supporting great communication between the shipper and final receiver.
  • Holding ourselves accountable to the shippers during the movement.
  • Providing accurate reporting with up-to-date information when tracking commodities in transit.

Key Benefits

The service provided includes investigating the movement thoroughly to establish the most cost effective routing that would suit the shipment, maintaining quality standards to ensure that damage and claims are avoided.

Single-point service, with a personalized approach
 

We can offer our services in part, or perform the whole operation on behalf of the shipper/receiver and manage it accordingly.
 

Vast experience in the field of logistics management to best serve the clients needs

The organization of ocean freight, stevedoring, barging, warehousing and terminals, trucking, rail and inventory management are all functions that we are very comfortable with and perform regularly.

Competitive solutions at acceptable pricing levels

GMI offers consolidated billing for the entire operation to its clients and then disburses to the various contracted companies on checked and correct invoices (thus reducing the administration costs associated with international trade).

Available 24/7/365 Service
 

Happy to provide notifications to keep our customers well informed of the cargo whereabouts and keep both the receivers and their appointed terminals well advised of the cargos arrival dates.

The logistical management services we offer our customers includes:

  Arranging and assisting in all of the facets of shipment documentation, including coordinating the Bills Of Lading       and Commercial Invoices for each shipment with the respective clearing agents.

  Arranging for Customs Clearing agents on our customers behalf, and ensuring that the customs releases get to           the shipping line agents before each shipment's port arrival and having all cargoes pre-cleared to avoid any port       and shipment delays/costs.

  Offering on the ocean and inland freight (barge/rail/road) on all shipments including Just-In-Time shipments or         shipments that need centralized warehousing for later distribution.

  Providing notifications which would keep our customers well informed of the cargo whereabouts and keep both         the receivers and their appointed terminals well advised of the cargo's arrival dates.

  Managing stock-levels from distribution centers providing accurate reports of cargo flows.
